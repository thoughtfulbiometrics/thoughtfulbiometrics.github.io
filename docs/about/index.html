In the novel, The Strange Case of Dr. Jekyll and Mr. Hyde, Mr. Poole is Dr. Jekyll’s virtuous and loyal butler. Similarly, Poole is an upstanding and effective butler that helps you build Jekyll themes. It’s made by @mdo.

There are currently two themes built on Poole:

  • Hyde
  • Lanyon

Learn more and contribute on GitHub.
